Who We Are

Building South Africa's
Future, Together

Located in Kuruman, Northern Cape, Merenton Enterprise Reengineering Consultancy is a Level 1 BBBEE Contributor and 100% Black-owned company founded in 2015 by Oageng Simon Mere.

Initially focused on taxation, financial management, payroll, HR, and business advisory services, Merenton broadened its scope in 2024 to encompass general building, civil works, steel works, cleaning services, and supply & distribution.
