Who We Are

Merenton Enterprise Reengineering Consultancy is a Level 1 BBBEE Contributor and a 100% Black-owned company founded in 2015 by Oageng Simon Mere.

Located in Kuruman town, we have established a strong reputation for providing high-quality services. Initially concentrating on taxation, financial management, payroll, HR, marketing, business coaching, and advisory services, Merenton broadened its services in 2024 to encompass general building, civil works, steel works, cleaning services, as well as the supply and distribution of various goods.

Backed by a skilled and dedicated team, we support clients from planning to project completion. Merenton is known for meeting deadlines, maintaining strong client relationships, and consistently delivering outstanding results across both professional and general service sectors.
