Our Story

Building Excellence
Since 2015

Merenton Enterprise Reengineering Consultancy was founded in 2015 by Oageng Simon Mere, a business consultant with more than 17 years of experience in small business development. Located in Kuruman, Northern Cape, we have built a strong reputation for delivering high-quality services.

Initially focusing on taxation, financial management, payroll, HR, and advisory services, Merenton expanded in 2024 to encompass general building, civil works, steel works, cleaning services, and supply & distribution — transforming into a truly multidisciplinary enterprise.

Our Journey

Key Milestones

From a specialized consultancy to a multidisciplinary powerhouse — a decade of growth and transformation.

2015

Company Foundation

Merenton Enterprise Reengineering Consultancy founded by Oageng Simon Mere, focusing on professional services including taxation, financial management, and business advisory.

2017

BBBEE Level 1 Accreditation

Achieved Level 1 BBBEE contributor status with 135% recognition level, cementing our position as a transformation leader in the industry.

2020

Expansion of Professional Services

Broadened our offering to include comprehensive HR management, marketing services, and specialized business development consulting for SMEs and corporates.

2024

Diversification into General Services

Successfully expanded our portfolio to include general building, civil works, steel works, cleaning services, and supply & distribution of goods.

Present

Multidisciplinary Service Provider

Operating as a comprehensive provider with a skilled team delivering both professional consulting and general contracting services across multiple sectors of South Africa's economy.

Founder & Director

Mr. Oageng Simon Mere

Sole Director & Shareholder · Kuruman, Northern Cape

An Economist, General Tax Practitioner, and Business Development Scholar with a BSc Agric and BSc Agric (Hons) in Agricultural Economics and Management Practice from the University of North West and University of Cape Town respectively.

With over 17 years of experience across academia, government, and the private sector, Mr. Mere has held key roles including Researcher, Lecturer, Economist, Business Advisor, and General Manager overseeing project implementation and evaluation.

Our Multidisciplinary Team

Merenton operates through a well-structured organisational framework designed to ensure efficient project execution and service delivery. The company is guided by Technical & Business Management, overseeing all core operations including stakeholder relations, financial management, procurement, and compliance.

Our team includes part-time qualified engineers, builders, and full-time business development professionals who ensure high-quality project planning, execution, and delivery.
