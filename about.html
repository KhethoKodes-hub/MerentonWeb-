Our Story

From a specialized consultancy to a comprehensive service provider

Building Excellence Since 2015

Merenton Enterprise Reengineering Consultancy was founded in 2015 by Oageng Simon Mere, a business consultant with more than 17 years of experience in small business development. Located in Kuruman town, we have established a strong reputation for providing high-quality services.

Initially concentrating on taxation, financial management, payroll, HR, marketing, business coaching, and advisory services, Merenton broadened its services in 2024 to encompass general building, civil works, steel works, cleaning services, as well as the supply and distribution of various goods.

Backed by a skilled and dedicated team, we support clients from planning to project completion. Merenton is known for meeting deadlines, maintaining strong client relationships, and consistently delivering outstanding results across both professional and general service sectors.

Our Multidisciplinary Team

Merenton Enterprise Reengineering Consultancy operates through a well-structured organizational framework designed to ensure efficient project execution and service delivery.

The company is guided by Technical & Business Management, which oversees all core operations including stakeholder relations, financial management, procurement, and compliance. Our team includes part-time qualified engineers, builders, and full-time business development professionals who ensure high-quality project planning, execution, and delivery.

For tender-based projects, the business consistently assembles a team of highly skilled professionals with specialized expertise to meet specific project requirements. This flexible approach allows us to deliver excellence across our diverse service portfolio.
