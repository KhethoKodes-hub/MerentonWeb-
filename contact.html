Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to the most common questions about getting in touch with Merenton.

What information should I include in my inquiry?

Please include your full name, contact details, the service you're interested in, and a brief description of your project requirements. The more detail you provide, the better we can tailor our response and provide an accurate quote.

How quickly will I receive a response?

We aim to respond to all inquiries within 24 hours on business days. For urgent matters, please call us directly at 083 573 7525 during business hours for immediate assistance.

Do you offer free consultations?

Yes, we offer free initial consultations for all our services. This allows us to understand your requirements properly and provide you with an accurate, competitive quote and tailored proposal.

What areas do you serve?

While based in Kuruman, we serve clients throughout the John Taolo Gaetsewe District and surrounding areas. For certain services, we can arrange to work in other regions depending on project scope and requirements.

Can I visit your office without an appointment?

While we welcome walk-in visitors during business hours, we recommend scheduling an appointment to ensure the appropriate team member is available for your specific needs. Use the contact form above or call ahead to book.
