Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common questions about contacting Merenton

What information should I include in my inquiry?

+

Please include your full name, contact details, the service you're interested in, and a brief description of your project requirements. The more details you provide, the better we can understand your needs and provide an accurate response.

How quickly will I receive a response?

+

We aim to respond to all inquiries within 24 hours during business days. For urgent matters, please call us directly at 083 573 7525 during business hours.

Do you offer free consultations?

+

Yes, we offer free initial consultations for all our services. This allows us to understand your requirements and provide you with an accurate quote and proposal.

What areas do you serve?

+

While based in Kuruman, we serve clients throughout the John Taolo Gaetsewe District and surrounding areas. For certain services, we can arrange to work in other regions depending on project scope and requirements.

Can I visit your office without an appointment?

+

While we welcome walk-in visitors, we recommend scheduling an appointment to ensure that the appropriate team member is available to assist you with your specific needs. You can call ahead or use the contact form to request a meeting.
